--- Correlations Under Validation ---

1. Briggs & Young (1963) - Heat Transfer
   Nu = 0.134 × Re_d^0.681 × Pr^(1/3) × (s/h_f)^0.2 × (s/t)^0.1134
   Valid Re_d range: 1,100 ~ 18,000

2. ESDU 86022 (1986) - Pressure Drop
   K_f = 4.567 × Re_d^(-0.242) × A_increase^0.504 × (S_T/d_o)^(-0.376) × (S_L/d_o)^(-0.546)
   Valid Re_d range: 100 ~ 100,000

--- CFD Results ---

Status: Pending (mesh quality issues)
Solver: chtMultiRegionSimpleFoam (OpenFOAM v1912)
Turbulence: Laminar

Issue: Complex fin geometry tetrahedral mesh causes numerical instability 
       in enthalpy equation (negative temperature divergence).
       
Solutions attempted:
  - Reduced relaxation factors (h: 0.5 → 0.01)
  - Conservative numerical schemes (upwind, limited)
  - Reduced solid-fluid temperature difference (50K → 20K)

Next steps:
  - Use structured mesh (snappyHexMesh)
  - Simplify fin geometry
  - 2D preliminary validation
